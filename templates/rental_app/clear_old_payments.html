What happens when you clear old payments?
  • All payment records older than the selected number of days will be permanently deleted
  • Tenant records will remain intact
  • Current tenant payment statuses will not be affected
  • This helps keep your database clean and improves performance
Before clearing old payments:
  • Consider exporting your data first using the "Export Data" feature
  • Make sure you have backups of important payment records
  • Choose an appropriate time period based on your business needs
